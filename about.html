Crafting Chairs for Every Space.

Alif Chairs started office furniture & institutional chairs business in year 1996. It is successfully running as family owned business since last 26 years.

We are empowered with state of the art facilities for manufacturing. Rigorous quality checks and accuracy in dimensions ensure the durability and performance of our products. We combine quality craftsmanship with modern technology.

We take pride in providing ergonomically sound and space efficient products that are cost effective for all kinds of business establishments.

All the raw materials used by us conform with Bifma standards. Being a trendsetter, we are continuously introducing new products to our product range.
